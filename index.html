Her Path: A Journey of Faith

Every life is a unique tapestry. Fatima's journey began in the vibrant district of Besiktas, Istanbul, and has been woven with threads of experience, challenge, and growth. This space honours her personal path, marked by resilience and transformation.

Central to her present is a deep and abiding Christian faith, a source of comfort, guidance, and unwavering hope. It shapes her perspective, fuels her kindness, and illuminates her days.

While acknowledging the past that has shaped her, the focus here is on the vibrant present and the hopeful future, celebrating the wonderful person she is today.

Rooted in Faith

Her Christian faith is not just a belief system, but a living foundation that permeates her daily life. It's found in quiet moments of reflection, in acts of service, and in the grace she extends to others.

It provides a lens through which to view the world – one of hope, forgiveness, and love. It's the anchor that holds steady through changing tides and the light that guides her way.

The Spice of Life: Passions & Pleasures

The Art of Breakfast

There's a special kind of magic in starting the day right. She possesses a wonderful talent for crafting delicious breakfasts, especially her perfectly made eggs with onions! It's an expression of care and a delightful way to begin the day.

Music for the Soul: Shakira

Music moves us, and the vibrant rhythms and heartfelt lyrics of Shakira resonate deeply. Her music brings energy, joy, and a soundtrack to many moments. Hips don't lie, and good music speaks volumes!

A Sweet Treat: Dondurma

Simple pleasures are often the best. A fondness for dondurma – that unique, chewy Turkish ice cream – is a delightful quirk. It's a taste of sweetness and perhaps a reminder of fun experiences.

The Joy of Tidiness

Finding peace and satisfaction in a clean and orderly space is a special kind of joy. She appreciates the calm that comes with tidiness and enjoys the process of making things sparkle.
